Frequently Asked Questions

  • what is website development ?

    Web development is the building and maintenance of websites; it's the work that happens behind the scenes to make a website look great.

  • What is Web Hosting ?

    Web hosting is service that enables you to upload and store your websites HTML and related files on a web server.

  • How much does a new website cost?

    There’s not one single answer to this question. A website design is quoted based on the needs of each individual project. Every website is unique and requires different components; we design and develop custom websites specifically for your small business. We’ll ask a lot of questions, assess your needs, and give you a quote based on that assessment. Most of our sites run in the ₹5,000 – ₹12,000 range, but can be more or less depending on needs.

  • Will my website be mobile-friendly?

    Absolutely! Having a mobile-friendly website is more important than ever! We work hard to ensure your website looks great on a variety of devices.

  • When do I pay?

    For most projects, equal payments are made at the start, midway, and the end of the project, but we can work with you to set up a schedule that meets your needs. We understand that this is a big investment and want to help you budget for the expense in whatever way possible. We accept checks, PayPal, and all major credit cards.

  • Who writes the content for the site?

    Usually that is you. You are the expert on your business, so it’s usually best if it comes from you. If you need help, we can recommend copywriters who can help clean up or write original content for you at an additional cost.
